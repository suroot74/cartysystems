How I work

This is not abstract consulting for the sake of slide decks. The goal is practical improvement.

Start with the real problem. Every engagement begins by understanding what is actually slowing the work down — whether that is a messy workflow, repetitive admin work, disconnected tools, weak documentation, or unclear communication.
